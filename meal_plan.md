# A Week of Gentle, Nourishing Meals for Nida

> Low fat | Medium fiber | Cooked veggies | Simple prep
> Tailored for: SLE, gastritis recovery, iron deficiency, fatigue

Meals marked **[Iron+]** pair an iron-rich food with vitamin C — that combo roughly triples iron absorption, which matters right now.

---

## 7-Day Meal Plan

### Monday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Oatmeal cooked in milk with banana slices and a spoon of peanut butter | 5 min |
| Lunch | Scrambled eggs on buttered toast with warm sauteed spinach, squeeze of lemon **[Iron+]** | 10 min |
| Dinner | One-pan baked salmon with orzo and zucchini (olive oil, lemon, salt) — make a double portion | 25 min |

### Tuesday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Greek yogurt with honey and a sliced kiwi | 2 min |
| Lunch | Leftover salmon + orzo, warmed with a splash of water | 5 min |
| Dinner | Thai-style chicken coconut soup: chicken breast, light coconut milk, ginger, mushrooms, rice noodles — skip the chili | 20 min |

### Wednesday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Soft-scrambled eggs with buttered toast and a clementine **[Iron+]** | 8 min |
| Lunch | Leftover coconut chicken soup | 5 min |
| Dinner | Pasta with olive oil, garlic, canned tuna, and steamed green beans — finish with a lemon squeeze **[Iron+]** | 15 min |

### Thursday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Oatmeal with banana and a sprinkle of chia seeds | 5 min |
| Lunch | Rice bowl with rotisserie chicken, steamed carrots, soy sauce, sesame oil | 10 min |
| Dinner | Baked cod with mashed potatoes and steamed peas, lemon butter over the fish | 25 min |

### Friday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Greek yogurt with honey and a few stewed berries (microwave 30 sec) | 3 min |
| Lunch | Leftover cod, mashed potato, peas | 5 min |
| Dinner | Lentil and carrot soup with crusty bread and butter **[Iron+]** | 20 min |

### Saturday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Scrambled eggs, toast, orange slices **[Iron+]** | 8 min |
| Lunch | Leftover lentil soup with bread | 5 min |
| Dinner | Mediterranean chicken and rice: pan-seared chicken thigh, basmati, sauteed zucchini and red bell pepper, olive oil, oregano | 25 min |

### Sunday
| Meal | What | Prep |
|---|---|---|
| Breakfast | Banana-peanut butter toast with a glass of milk | 5 min |
| Lunch | Leftover chicken and rice | 5 min |
| Dinner | Buttered pasta with flaked smoked mackerel or canned sardines, steamed spinach, lemon **[Iron+]** | 15 min |

## Grocery List

### Proteins
- Salmon fillets — 2 (~150g each)
- Cod fillets — 2 (~150g each)
- Chicken breast — 1 (for Thai soup)
- Chicken thighs, boneless — 2–3
- Rotisserie chicken — 1 small (shortcut for tired days)
- Canned tuna in water — 1 can
- Canned sardines or smoked mackerel — 1 tin
- Canned lentils — 1–2 cans
- Eggs — 1 dozen

### Grains / Carbs
- Rolled oats — small bag
- Sliced bread (soft whole grain)
- Orzo — 1 small box
- Pasta (spaghetti or linguine) — 1 box
- Basmati or jasmine rice — small bag
- Rice noodles (thin) — 1 pack
- Crusty bread / small baguette
- Potatoes — 4–5 medium

### Vegetables (all cooked)
- Zucchini — 3
- Green beans — 1 small bag (fresh or frozen)
- Frozen peas — 1 bag
- Spinach — 1 bag (frozen is easier)
- Carrots — 1 small bag
- Mushrooms — 1 small pack
- Red bell pepper — 2
- Garlic — 1 head
- Fresh ginger — 1 small knob
- Lemons — 3

### Fruit
- Bananas — 4–5
- Kiwis — 3
- Clementines or oranges — 4–5
- Frozen mixed berries — 1 small bag

### Dairy
- Milk — 1 small carton
- Greek yogurt — 1 tub (plain)
- Butter — 1 small pack
- Parmesan — small wedge (optional)

### Pantry / Sauces
- Olive oil
- Sesame oil — small bottle
- Soy sauce (low sodium)
- Light coconut milk — 1 can
- Chicken or veg stock — 1 carton
- Honey
- Peanut butter — small jar (smooth)
- Chia seeds — small bag (optional)
- Dried oregano

### Nice to Have
- Microwave rice pouches — 2–3 (backup for exhausted evenings)
- Ginger tea bags (soothing for gastritis)
- Dark chocolate, 70% — a small bar (magnesium + mood)

## No-Prep Snacks

- Greek yogurt with honey
- Banana with peanut butter
- Clementine or kiwi
- Plain crackers with a slice of cheese
- Hard-boiled eggs (boil a few on Sunday, last 5 days)
- Warm chicken broth in a mug — restorative on bad days
- Oatcakes with a thin layer of peanut butter
- A square of dark chocolate with a glass of milk

---

## Quick Tips for the Shop

- If the full list feels like too much, prioritise:
  **eggs, oats, salmon, chicken, rice, pasta, frozen spinach, frozen peas, bananas, lemons, olive oil, yogurt, bread**
- Frozen vegetables = no washing, no chopping, no guilt
- Boil 4 eggs Sunday evening — breakfast handled for two mornings
- One rotisserie chicken covers two dinners easily
- Sip ginger tea between meals (not with them) — helps the gastritis

---

*This plan is a scaffold, not a rulebook. On low-energy days, even just toast with eggs and an orange is a win.*
